Punjabi music is a genre that blends traditional Punjabi folk music with contemporary pop and hip-hop elements. It is characterized by upbeat rhythms, catchy melodies, and lyrics that often touch on themes of love, partying, gang activities and the like. The genre has gained popularity in recent years, particularly among youth from all over India, also being incorporated into Bollywood movies, and has produced several successful artists who have achieved mainstream success both in India and abroad.
